Welcome: About Us

Educating The Children is a UK registered charity that focuses on empowering young women through education and training that increases their employability. In 2019, we launched Code Queen in Kampala which is an all-female bootcamp designed to train young women in coding and soft skills. Code Queen has successfully run 3 cohorts and graduated 70 students since the launch in 2019.

Mission

To empower girls and young women in East Africa through education and training.


Vision

To increase capacity to be able to serve 1,000 women a year by 2025.
